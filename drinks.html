Drinks

Rum Runner

  • 1 oz light rum
  • 1 oz medium or dark rum
  • 1 oz banana liqueur
  • 1/2 oz blackberry liqueur
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 oz lime juice (squeezed)
  • 1/2 oz grenadine
  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker and shake well
  2. Pour in glass and garnish (with pineapple slice and cherry)
