Rum Runner

A boozy banana flavored drink that'll make you feel like you're on vacation.
  • 1 oz light rum
  • 1 oz medium or dark rum
  • 1 oz banana liqueur
  • 1/2 oz blackberry liqueur
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 oz lime juice (squeezed)
  • 1/2 oz grenadine
  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and shake well
  2. Pour in glass and garnish (with pineapple slice and cherry, or one of the used limes)

Nico Colada

A simple poor-mans Pina Colada on the rocks. Can be flavored however you like!
  • 1.5-2 oz medium rum
  • Half of bottle of Sparkling Ice drink (Pineapple for basic Pina Colada, but it works with any flavor)
  • .75 oz Coffee Mate Coconut flavored creamer
  1. Add ice to a cup
  2. Pour in the rum, then Sparking Ice, then top off with creamer
  3. Stir with a spoon, it should foam up (Add pineapple wedge for garnish)

Lemon Drop

A combination of boozy, tart, and sweet with citrus and sugar!
  • 2 oz vodka
  • .5 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • Sugar
  1. Add the vodka, triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice
  2. Rub the remains of the lemon around the rim of the glass, then coat in sugar
  3. Shake all ingredients, then strain into glass

Thanksippin'

A nice cocktail perfect for a Thanksgiving Feast.
  • 1.5 oz Cinnamon infused vodka
  • .75 oz Triple sec
  • .75 oz Cranberry Juice
  • .75 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Maple Syrup
  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice
  2. Shake well
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass
  4. Add a cinnamon stick on top or inside for garnish
